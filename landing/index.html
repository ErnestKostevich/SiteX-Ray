FAQ

Questions, honestly answered

Still curious? Email ernest2011kostevich@gmail.com.

How is this different from PageSpeed Insights?
PageSpeed gives you a Performance number and tells you to "minify CSS." SiteX-Ray reads your actual content, your CTAs, your value proposition, and gives you specific rewrites and structural changes a human consultant would. Think of PageSpeed as a doctor saying "your blood pressure is 145." SiteX-Ray is the doctor saying "stop drinking coffee at 4pm and walk 20 minutes a day."
Is the AI actually any good, or generic fluff?
We run frontier large language models against a custom 200-line auditor prompt built from real conversion-audit checklists. Every finding cites evidence from your actual site — your real title, your real CTAs, your real meta tags. Vague advice like "improve your content" is explicitly banned in the prompt. If you ever get a report that feels generic, reply to the email and we'll re-run it for free.
What if I don't like the report?
Full refund within 7 days, no questions asked. Just email us. We'd rather have you happy than your $39.
Do you store my data?
We process your URL through our AI provider to generate the report, then email it to you. We don't store URLs or reports. USDT payments are verified on-chain via public blockchain APIs — we never see your wallet keys.
How do I pay with USDT?
Click "Get full audit — $39", choose TRC-20 (TRON) or ERC-20 (Ethereum), send exactly 39 USDT to the address shown, then paste your transaction hash. We verify on-chain (must be within 24 hours) and email your full report automatically.
How fresh is the audit? Will it work next month?
Every audit is generated live from your current homepage. If you change your site, run a new audit. We recommend re-running quarterly after big content updates.
Can you audit pages other than the homepage?
MVP is homepage-only — that's where 80% of conversion issues live. Multi-page audits are on the roadmap. Email us if you need a specific page audited and we'll do it manually.
I'm a web agency — can I resell this?
Yes. We're piloting a partnership program: wholesale audits + your branding. Email us with "Partnership" in the subject.

With your own key we deliver the full 10–15 page Claude Sonnet audit (not the 1-page teaser), and you skip the $39 paid tier entirely — you pay your own Anthropic bill instead (a few cents per audit). Your key is used once for that single audit and never stored. Useful for agencies and devs running many audits.
