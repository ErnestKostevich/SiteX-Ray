# Post-purchase email sequence

After a $39 sale, send these emails over 7 days. Goal: deliver value beyond the report, build the relationship, set up the upsell to "Done-for-you" ($299+).

Use Cloudflare Email Service (reports@sitexray.xyz) or send manually from Gmail for the first 20 customers.

---

## Email 1 — Day 0 (automatic, delivered with the report)

**Subject:** Your SiteX-Ray report — [their domain]

```
Hi [first name],

Your full audit for [their domain] is attached / below. Quick reading guide:

→ Scroll to "Quick wins" first — those are the 30-minute fixes that move the needle fastest.

→ "Critical issues" are the things genuinely costing you money right now. Fix in the next 2 weeks.

→ "90-day plan" is the strategic stuff. Don't try to do it all at once.

One ask: when you've shipped 2–3 of the quick wins, reply and let me know what changed. Real outcomes help me improve the tool, and I'll happily run a free re-audit 30 days from now to show the difference.

— [Your name]
SiteX-Ray

PS — if you want me to implement the fixes for you, hit reply with "DFY". We do it for a flat $299 and you have a fixed site in 5 business days. 7-day money-back if nothing measurably improves.
```

This is the **most important email** — it's where DFY upsells happen.

## Email 2 — Day 3

**Subject:** Have you tried fix #1 yet?

```
[First name],

Quick check-in: have you (or your dev) shipped any of the quick wins from your report yet?

The single highest-leverage one is usually [pick from their report — meta description, primary CTA, page title]. Takes 10 minutes.

If you've tried and it didn't work, reply with the question — I'll usually have an answer same-day.

If you haven't gotten to it: forward the report to whoever updates your site. You'll get to it eventually but momentum matters.

— [Your name]
```

**Subject:** Want me to just do this for you?

```
[First name],

A week ago you bought a SiteX-Ray audit for [their domain].

Most people read the report, agree it's accurate, then it sits in the inbox while they get busy. That's fine — but here's the alternative:

**Done-for-you SiteX-Ray: $299, 5 business days, fixed site.**

What I do:
- Fix every quick win in your report (titles, meta, alt text, CTAs)
- Rewrite weak copy where the audit flagged it
- Implement the structural changes (hero, navigation, social proof)
- Send before-and-after report + new audit score

7-day money-back if your audit score doesn't go up by at least 20 points.

Reply "DFY" and I'll send a 1-line questionnaire and a payment link. Done in a week.

Or — if the audit was enough and you're shipping fixes yourself, awesome. Reply with what changed, I'll re-audit free at the 30-day mark.

— [Your name]
```

## Email 4 — Day 30 (only if they haven't taken DFY)

**Subject:** Free re-audit for [their domain]?

```
[First name],

It's been a month since your SiteX-Ray report. Want me to re-audit on the house, show the before/after?

No catch — just curious whether the fixes moved the score. Reply YES and I'll send a fresh report by tomorrow.

— [Your name]
```

This email both:
- Re-engages dormant customers (15% will reply, ~5% will buy DFY here)
- Builds your case-study library ("Customer X went from 38 → 71 in 30 days")

## Refund handling (rare but important)

If someone asks for a refund within 7 days, **just refund**. No questions, no friction. Lemon Squeezy makes this one-click.

Reply:
```
Done — refunded just now, should hit your account in 3–5 business days. 
Any feedback on what didn't work? Genuinely useful for me to know.
— [Your name]
```

A no-friction refund policy:
- Eliminates "is this legit?" anxiety on the landing page
- Refund rate at $39 will be <5% in practice
- The 95% who keep it are happier than they'd otherwise be

Testimonials at this stage are gold — paste them straight on your landing.
