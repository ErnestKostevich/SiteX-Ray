# Cold email playbook

LinkedIn DMs get more replies. Cold email scales bigger and is harder to ignore. Use both. Email is especially good for *follow-up* after a DM that went nowhere, since you reach the actual inbox.

---

## Setup

- Use a separate email for outreach — don't burn your main inbox's deliverability. E.g. `ernest@sitexray.xyz` once you have a domain.
- Warm the inbox for 1 week before mass-sending (use a free tool like Mailwarm.com free trial, or just send 5–10 personal emails a day from it for a week).
- **Cap at 50 cold emails per day per sending address.** Above that, Gmail/Outlook flag spam.
- Always include a one-click unsubscribe (legally required in most countries — CAN-SPAM in US, GDPR in EU). Plain text "Reply STOP and I'll never email you again" works.

---

## Subject lines (test 2–3 in rotation)

1. `Quick note about your homepage`
2. `Found 3 things on [domain] that might be costing you leads`
3. `[Their first name] — saw your site, 60-sec note`
4. `[Domain] — homepage audit (free)`
5. `Honest question about [domain]`

**Avoid:** "RE:" or "FW:" fake-reply tricks (deceptive, hurts trust). Avoid emoji and ALL CAPS. Keep under 7 words.

---

## Template A — opener with a teaser finding

```
Subject: Found 3 things on yourcompany.com that might be costing you leads

Hi [First name],

I ran a free audit on yourcompany.com this morning and noticed:

1. Your homepage title is "[their actual title]" — generic titles like that 
   typically cost 15–25% of search clicks vs a specific one.

2. There's no phone number above the fold. For [their niche], that usually 
   means 30–40% fewer inbound calls.

3. No reviews or testimonials visible on the homepage. In [their niche], 
   social proof above the fold typically lifts conversion 20–35%.

I built the report into a clean 5-page PDF. Want me to send it over? 
Free, no upsell.

— [Your first name]
[Your title], SiteX-Ray
Reply STOP and I'll never bug you again.
```

## Follow-up sequence (use if no reply)

### Day +3
```
Subject: re: Found 3 things on yourcompany.com

Hi [First name],

Just bumping this in case it got buried. The audit's a 5-min read and 
the fixes are 30 minutes of work, no developer needed. Want me to send 
it?

— [Your first name]
```

### Day +7 — break-up email
```
Subject: re: yourcompany.com — last note

Hi [First name],

Last email from me, promise. If audits aren't a priority, all good — 
but here's the single biggest issue from your homepage in case it's 
useful:

> [paste the headline_finding from the teaser]

That one alone is worth a fix.

Good luck,
[Your first name]
```

## Reply-rate expectations

Cold email industry average: 1–3% reply. With personalized observations from a real audit: 5–10% reply. Of replies, ~30% convert to a free teaser. Of free teasers, ~10% convert to $39.

**Funnel math:** 250 emails/week → 12 replies → 4 teasers delivered → 0.5 sales/week.

Combine with LinkedIn DMs to compound.

## Tools (all free tier)

- **Apollo.io** — find emails of decision-makers (free: 50 emails/day)
- **Hunter.io** — verify email deliverability (free: 25 verifications/month)
- **Mailtrap / Smartlead** — schedule + track outbound (Smartlead has a 14-day trial)
- **Your Gmail** — send manually from a dedicated address (no tool needed at <50/day volume)

---

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Buy a list of 10,000 emails and blast — your domain gets blacklisted, recoverable but painful
- ❌ Send the entire audit report in the email — too long, no one reads, looks spammy
- ❌ Use template variables like `{firstname}` that fail to substitute (every recipient knows you mass-sent)
- ❌ Mention price ($39) in the cold email — wait until they're hooked. The pitch is "free audit"
- ❌ Use clickbait subject lines — works once, kills your sender reputation forever
- ❌ Email anyone in CIS/RU markets — payment + currency friction kills the conversion (per our targeting)
